# Development Checklist

- Is the code readable and maintainable?
- Are names accurate, specific, and consistent?
- Are errors handled intentionally with actionable messages?
- Is business logic duplicated across modules or endpoints?
- Are functions and classes appropriately sized and focused?
- Are complex branches documented or simplified?
- Are TODOs masking important technical debt?
- Are dependencies used coherently without unnecessary indirection?
- Is public behavior documented for future maintainers?
- Are low-confidence observations clearly marked as hypotheses?
