# AI Checklist

- Prompt injection, tool abuse, or unsafe context-window composition risks
- Validation and normalization of model inputs, files, and retrieved context
- Output sanitization, policy filtering, and human-review checkpoints
- Hallucination mitigation, confidence signaling, and fallback behavior
- Training-data or retrieval-data poisoning exposure
- Bias, fairness, or high-impact decision documentation
- Model versioning, evaluation traceability, and rollback controls
