# Data Privacy Checklist

- What personal data, identifiers, or sensitive attributes are collected, inferred, or stored?
- Is each data element necessary for the stated purpose (data minimization)?
- Is data lineage clear from collection through storage, processing, export, and deletion?
- Are consent, opt-in/opt-out, or lawful-basis signals visible where applicable?
- Are retention periods, deletion paths, and archival behavior defined and enforced?
- Can users exercise deletion, correction, export, or access rights without manual guesswork, including right-to-erasure workflows?
- Do logs, analytics, error reports, or traces capture personal data unnecessarily?
- Is production data prevented from leaking into test, staging, analytics, or developer environments without anonymization?
- Are third-party processors, outbound integrations, or data-sharing paths explicit and minimized?
- Are data-localization, residency, or cross-border transfer requirements identified and enforced where relevant?
- Are consent state changes captured and respected across storage, processing, and downstream integrations?
- Are GDPR/CCPA signals present in docs, config, or product flows where relevant?
