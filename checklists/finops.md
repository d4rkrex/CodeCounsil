# FinOps Checklist

- Right-sizing of compute, database, and cache resources
- Autoscaling, scale-to-zero, or schedule-based scaling opportunities
- High-cost data transfer, egress, cross-region, or replication patterns
- Orphaned, duplicate, or always-on resources lacking ownership signals
- Resource tagging for cost allocation, environment, team, and lifecycle
- Storage tiering, retention, and lifecycle policies for logs, backups, and artifacts
- Reserved-instance, savings-plan, or committed-use opportunities
